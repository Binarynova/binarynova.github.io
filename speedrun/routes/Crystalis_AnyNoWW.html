Crystalis Any%

Based on DragondarchSDA's route.

Name character. Start timer on name entry.

Leaf Area

  • Get money.
  • Use shop glitch to buy Alarm Flute (optional: Antidote).
  • Get Sword of Wind.
  • Kill things on the way to Zebu's Cave.
  • Talk to Zebu.
  • Kill things on the way to sleeping guard.
  • In cave, equip Alarm Flute.
  • Bump into guard, press B twice. Mash through text.
  • Equip Windmill Key.
  • Step into Windmill and immediately press B to use Key. Then leave.
  • Grind to level 2 using blue slimes outside windmill cave. When the blue slime gets you to 22, leave and do one last loop outside to hit level 2 right in front of the cave.
  • Get Refresh. "Cave to Brynmaer" split.

Cave to Brynmaer

  • Get and equip Ball of Wind.
  • Optional: get medical herb.
  • Grind up to level 3.

BOSS: Vampire.

  • Get Rabbit Boots.

Brynmaer Area

  • Get statue of onyx.
  • Head to Brynmaer and give statue to Akahana.
  • Equip Gas Mask as Akahana is leaving.
  • Go to the Inn.
  • Use shop glitch to get 5 or 6 free pairs of warp boots.
  • Kill axe throwers on the way to Oak.

Oak

  • Enter Oak.
  • Use warp boots back to Brynmaer.

Brynmaer Area

  • Kill axe guys on the way to Tornel's Training Center.
  • Equip Warp Boots before facing Stom.
  • Warp Boots will trigger during fight, go back to Leaf.
  • Warp back to Oak.

Oak Area

  • Talk to mother. Go into forest to get child.
  • Talk to child and warp to Oak.
  • Talk to mother to get insect flute.
  • Talk to elder to get Sword of Fire. Split.
  • Go to Inn.
  • Leave forest and grind to level 4 with approx. 70xp to go to level 5 on Blue mushrooms.

BOSS: Giant Insect

  • Get and equip Ball of Fire.
  • Equip Rabbit Boots, and warp to Brynmaer.

Brynmaer Area

  • Go to Inn.
  • Level on Blue Mushrooms on the way to Mt. Sabre - West.

Mt. Sabre - West

  • Kill ice zombies on lower area, then bunny hop up top left slope.
  • Take east cave. Switch between Wind and Fire to kill enemies and destroy walls.
  • Get Tornado Bracelet.
  • Head back outside to main loop.
  • Grind up to 400+ xp. Enter lower-right cave.
  • Route: Right, right, right, up.
  • Get Magic Ring and speak to Tornel to get Teleport. "Teleport" split.
  • Warp back to Oak.

Oak (and outside swamp)

  • Go to Inn.
  • Leave Oak.
  • Hit level 6 just before entering North Mt. Saber.

Mt. Sabre - North

  • Notification skip position (note the position of the hero's shadow next to the small snow pile):
  • Enter Inn to add to teleport screen.
  • Equip Rabbit Boots.
  • Alert guards, then bunny hop into entrance.
  • Go south at only split.
  • Grind on purple enemies until level 7.
  • Cave route after grind:
  • Right, up stairs, up, (outside), first cave, up second ice wall for key, back to first ice wall.
  • Make sure you're level 7.

BOSS: General Kelbesque - Spam sword attacks when he stands still.

  • Get Flame Bracelet.

Portoa

  • Rest at Inn.
  • Optional: Get free Platinum Shield and Bronze Armor.

Waterfall Cave

  • Cave route: right, (skip statues using glitch), right, up, right.
  • Get Sword of Water.
  • Use Paralysis and Sword of Water to kill Medusa-like enemies.
  • Grind until you have 1500xp, then warp to Portoa.

Portoa Area

  • Go to Inn.
  • Head south of Portoa to get Ball of Water.
  • Kill 3 axe throwers on the way.
  • Immediately leave and go east across water to cave.
  • Kill 1 axe thrower on the way. Ding 8!

Portoa Area Cave 1

  • Cave Route: Down at only split.
  • Fire Sword to attack, Wind for walls.
  • Don't bother switching to wind for the dragons.
  • Get Kirisa Plant.
  • Warp back to Portoa.
  • Go to cave East of Portoa.

Portoa Area Cave 2

  • Cave Route: up, right, down, down, right, left, down.
  • Get Fog Lamp.
  • Warp back to Portoa.

Portoa

  • Speak with Asina.
  • Use Medical Herb on dolphin.
  • Warp back to Portoa.
  • Go to Inn.
  • Give Fog Lamp to guy.
  • Take boat to Ocean.

Ocean Area

  • Talk to Kensu in building.
  • Call dolphin. Split.
  • Enter town of Joel to add to teleport list.
  • Find love pendant in cave behind Portoa.
  • Grind on jellyfish until you are level 10 with 7800xp.
  • Kill octopus thing north of grinding cave.
  • Use rabbit boots/dolphin glitch to fly over waterfall.
  • Go just behind whirlpool to get Barrier spell.

Swan

  • In Swan, speak with Stom in hut.
  • Use Paralysis on kensu twice to learn Change spell.
  • Teleport to Brynmaer.
  • Head to Amazones.

Amazones

  • Cast Change before entering town to transform into a woman.
  • Use Kirisa Plant on the Queen to get Bow of the Moon.
  • Go downstairs for Blizzard Bracelet.
  • Teleport to Swan.

Goa Area

  • Go to Inn.
  • Change into guard to leave Swan.
  • Enter Goa to add to teleport list.
  • Change to Stom to enter. Head to Shyron.

Shyron

  • Get Battle Shield and Ceramic Suit for free.
  • Talk to Zebu.
  • Equip Armor and Shield and Sword of Water.
  • Teleport to Shyron.

Styx

  • Leave and head north to Styx.
  • Use key on gate and enter.
  • Route: left, (grind on magician until lv 12 16k xp), left, right, across water x2, right, right, right.
  • Get Sword of Thunder.
  • After meeting teleport to Goa.

Goa Area and Shyron

  • Go to Inn.
  • Head into Goa castle.
  • Get message from Shyron and teleport there.

BOSS: Mado

  • Teleport to Goa.
  • Head south to desert.

Desert Area

  • Go into Oasis Cave.
  • Route: right, up, across water, down, right, up, across water, up, across water, down, across water, down.
  • Get Power ring.
  • Teleport to Goa.

Goa and Draygonia Castle

  • Go to Inn.
  • Head into Draygonia Castle.
  • Paralyse and kill enemies on the way to boss to hit level 13.
  • Route: left, left, first staircase of third set, up, right.

BOSS RE-FIGHT: General Kelbesque

  • Route: down, across 2nd water, right, across water, ignore next two water crossings, cross water, up.

BOSS RE-FIGHT?: Sabera

  • Route: right, down to get Magic Ring. Back up, left, left, down, left to next Magic Ring.
  • Grind on spiders near magic ring until level 14.
  • Route from spiders: down, right, right, up all the spikes, cross three moving platforms down, and one up.

BOSS RE-FIGHT: Mado

  • Route: left, right, up, right, left, down for magic ring, back up, left, up.

BOSS: Karmine

  • Get Storm Bracelet.
  • Route: down, left, up.
  • Use ivory statue on Kensu slime.
  • Learn Flight.
  • Warp to Shyron.

Mt. Hydra

  • Head into caves. Use flight to avoid some enemies.
  • Route from Shyron: up into first cave, left, right into second cave, left, right.
  • Get Bow of the Sun.
  • Warp to Goa.

Desert

  • Head south to desert.
  • Fly into cave with whirlpools.
  • Go to Sahara Inn.
  • Head east to cave.
  • Use flight to help kill scorpions.
  • Grind to 15 and 34500xp. (or all the way to level 16)
  • Head to pyramid.

Pyramid

  • Route: left, right, right, up (get magic ring), left, up, right, up stairs.

BOSS: Emperor Draygon

Pyramid (Back Entrance)

  • Leave Pyramid and go to back entrance.
  • Use Bow of Moon and Sun on statues.
  • Route: right, up, left, fly over two moving platforms.
  • Make sure you're level 16.
  • Use bow of truth to reveal Draygon's true form.

BOSS: Emperor Draygon Round 2 - Using Sword of Thunder and Barrier spell.

Tower

  • Gather enemies close together, use sword of thunder level 3 to clear floors.
  • Use Magic Rings to refill MP as needed.
  • Get Crystalis.
  • Go up stairs.

BOSS: DYNA

  • Walk in circles. Split when you stop moving.
