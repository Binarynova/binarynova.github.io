Final Fantasy 3 (No Job Glitch)

by Binarynova

The story of four young children who fall into a dark cave. There they find strangely colored water and decide to drink it. Little did they know the water would cause them to hallucinate about flying in airships, talking with strange characters, dressing in magical outfits, casting spells, and killing evil... clouds?
