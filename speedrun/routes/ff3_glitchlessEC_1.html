Final Fantasy 3 (Glitchless w/Encounter Skips)

by Binarynova

This is the story of four young children who, while exploring, fall into a dark cave. After being injured during a fight with goblins they find strangely colored water and decide to drink it, hoping it will heal them. Little did they know the water would cause them to hallucinate about flying in airships, meeting strange characters, dressing in magical outfits, casting spells, and killing evil... clouds?
