YS 1 Speedrun Guide (goal: sub 50 min)  

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qw6Y5C4Z6AE

---

Time starts on Difficulty selection. Skip the cutscene asap with B. Speed through all dialogue by holding B. Avoid ALL enemies unless specifically grinding. Safety save often! On bosses, split when health refills.

Ys 1

Get Money, Go Shopping

  1. Once you get control, go straight to Minea.
  2. Buy a Wing.
  3. Leave Minea, go north twice, east, south. Get Golden Vase. Use Wing.
  4. Sell Vase.
  5. Buy Sapphire Ring, and another Wing.
  6. Talk to three guys at bar, then one-eyed man again.
  7. Buy chain mail, small shield, and long sword. Equip.

Get Equipped With Quest

  1. Speak with Sara.
  2. Go to Zepik Village. Speak with Jeba.
  3. Grind two levels outside Shrine (until required xp = 798) Safety save.

Shrine Part 1

  1. Kill Jenocres.
  2. Go to second basement.
  3. Get treasure box key, and prison key.
  4. Return to first basement.
  5. Kill enemies outside of Feena’s cell.
  6. Release Feena, Get Mask.
  7. On ground floor, get Shield Ring and equip.
  8. Escort Feena to Zepik Village.
  9. Return to Shrine.

Shrine Part 2

  1. Go straight to Basement 3, get Ivory Key.
  2. Use 2nd golden statue, get Silver Shield, Equip.
  3. Go back and use 1st golden statue, equip Mask to open doorway near silver statue.
  4. Use 2nd golden statue, get Marble Key.
  5. Go back to recently skipped statue. Safety save.
  6. Kill Nyghtilger.
  7. Get book; Use Wing.

Abandoned Mine Part 1

  1. Buy Wing.
  2. Go to Sara’s shop, learn sara’s fate
  3. Go to abandoned mine.
  4. On Basement 1, head left, take first down, first right, first up.
  5. Get Silver Armor.
  6. Head south from armor to go to Basement 2.
  7. On Basement 2, go immediately right, then down and left. Bump enemy in door.
  8. Get Heal Ring.
  9. Go left, then down, then take third left.
  10. Get Silver Harmonica.
  11. Go right, then up, the second left, follow path.
  12. Get Roda Tree Seed.
  13. Use Wing.

Get Equipped With Silver Sword

  1. Buy Wing and Mirror.
  2. Talk to Rhea on city wall.
  3. Go to southern Roda tree, use seed, talk to tree.
  4. Get Silver Sword.

Abandonded Mine Part 2

  1. Head back to Abandoned Mine.
  2. Go straight to Basement 3.
  3. On Basement 3, follow mostly linear path. Ignore first fork up, take second.
  4. Get Darm Key
  5. Go back to following linear path, take next left.
  6. Get Heal Potion.
  7. Head right from previous fork.
  8. At this corner, grind XP on scorpion things to max level. Save after first level up.
  9. Continue following path to boss door.
  10. Safety Save!
  11. Kill Vaguillion.
  12. Get Book.
  13. Use Wing.

Tower Prep

  1. Head straight to Zepik village. Talk to Jeba and Feena.
  2. Leave Zepik and re-enter.
  3. Talk to Feena on dock.
  4. Talk to Jeba, ask her to read books.
  5. Leave Zepik and return.
  6. At Jeba’s house, speak with Feena then Jeba.
  7. Go to Thieves’ Hideout, talk with theives boss.
  8. Enter Darm Tower.
